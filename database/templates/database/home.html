Since retirement, I became a Research Associate stationed at the National Museum of Natural History, Smithsonian Institution, Washington, DC, USA. And I continue to do research on the Lepidoptera, primarily on the Gelechioidea.

My ongoing projects include: plylogenetics of the world Blastobasidae, including evolution of host-plant preferences and biogeography; Moths of America north of Mexico (MONA) fascicle on Blastobasidae, and many alpha-taxonomic and life-history studies on Nearctic, Neotropical, and Indo-Asian Gelechioidea.

I am and remain eager to collaborate with anyone who desires identifications of taxa within my expertise, including life-history studies or general collecting.
